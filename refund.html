You can cancel your subscription at any time from your Lemon Squeezy customer portal. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period — you will not be charged again after cancellation.

Partial Refunds

Processing Time

Approved refunds are processed within 3–5 business days. The refund will be credited to your original payment method.
